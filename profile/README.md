## 📊 Case Studies

### Case Study: Shelf Analytics for Retail Chains

**The Problem:**
Large retail chains struggle with:
- 8-12% out-of-stock rates costing billions in lost revenue
- Manual shelf audits taking 40+ hours per store weekly
- Poor planogram compliance (avg 60-70%)
- No real-time visibility into on-shelf availability

**Market Context:**
- Global retail analytics market: $8.5B (2024) → projected $18B (2029)
- 73% of retailers cite inventory visibility as top priority
- Labor costs for manual audits: $15-25/hour per store

**Our Solution:**
AI-powered shelf monitoring using computer vision:
- Real-time SKU detection with 95%+ accuracy
- Automatic out-of-stock alerts within 15 minutes
- Planogram compliance scoring
- Integration with existing POS and inventory systems

**Impact:**
- 65% reduction in out-of-stock incidents
- 90% reduction in audit labor costs
- 40% improvement in planogram compliance
- ROI achieved within 6 months

### Case Study: Autonomous Agent Teams for SaaS Operations

**The Problem:**
SaaS companies face:
- Engineering teams spending 30% time on routine maintenance
- Slow incident response (avg 2-4 hours MTTR)
- Knowledge silos and bus factor risks
- Scaling challenges with human-only operations

**Market Context:**
- AIOps market growing 25% CAGR
- Average cost of downtime: $5,600/minute
- Developer productivity loss: $85B annually (global)

**Our Solution:**
Gas Town — A multi-agent system where AI agents collaborate:
- **Mayor** — Strategic planning and orchestration
- **Deacon** — Code review and quality assurance
- **Witness** — Testing and verification
- **Refinery** — Dependency management and builds
- **Crew** — Parallel task execution

**Impact:**
- 70% reduction in routine maintenance time
- 5-minute MTTR for common issues
- 24/7 autonomous operations
- Self-improving through operational learnings
